Interests

It would be fair to call me a technology fan-boy. I will always be grateful that I have been able to create a career out of what has always been a hobby since very early on. I feel like everyday I stumble across some new kind of technology that I have to get my hands on. Here is what has been keeping me busy lately...

3D Printing

How can anyone resist being able to fast-prototype something two feet from the computer you designed it on. I have been following the hobby grade 3D printer from the early days of the RepRap project. I am currently building my own delta 3d printer.

Battle Robots

I like to think of battle bots as the high brow demolition derby. Since the first match I saw in my later high school years I have wanted to participate in a fighting league. I have a one-wheel design which is not unlike a ball mouse. I hope for it to one day see combat.

Home Automation

Even after seeing Hal 9000 misbehave in space, having a home-brewed automated home has been an active pursuit of mine. So far I have weather controlled cabinet lighting, with a side of thermostat control.
